Scenic Journey

Dome to Dome

The most cinematic 92km you will ever drive.

Duration 1 Day
Difficulty Easy
Season Mar – Jan
Altitude 3,200m

Overview

The Drive

Most guests arrive at YOLO Outdoors as part of a longer Ladakh trip and treat this drive as transit. Don't. The Manali to Jispa drive through the Atal Tunnel, past Sissu, along the Bhaga River through Keylong — is a journey worth experiencing consciously.

Stop by Stop

Route Guide

Morning Depart Manali +

Leave by 8am. Optional Hadimba Temple visit (30 min). Brief stop at Solang Valley.

9:30am Atal Tunnel +

9.02km. The world's longest highway single-tube tunnel at 10,000 ft. The landscape on the other side changes completely.

10:30am Sissu +

Tea and breakfast at a local dhaba. Sissu waterfall across the river is spectacular.

11:30am Tandi +

Chandra and Bhaga rivers meet here to form the Chenab. A geological moment worth 10 minutes.

12:30pm Keylong +

District capital of Lahaul and Spiti. Lunch. Optional Shashur Monastery visit.

2:00pm Jispa +

Arrive at YOLO Outdoors. Afternoon by the Bhaga River. Evening: watch the light die on the peaks.

Key Highlights

What You Will See

🚇 The tunnel portal — entering Lahaul for the first time
🏔 Valley floor drops and widens beyond Sissu
🌊 Bhaga River running beside you for 50km
📐 The scale — everything gets bigger after the tunnel

March through January. The Atal Tunnel keeps this route open almost year-round.
