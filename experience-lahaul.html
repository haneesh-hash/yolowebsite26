A Scenic Getaway in Lahaul

The Adventure

Embark on an exhilarating jeep safari to one of the highest mountain passes in the world – Baralacha La. Experience the rugged beauty of Lahaul as you navigate through the rocky terrain and snow-capped peaks.

Marvel at the changing landscapes as you journey from lush green valleys to barren high-altitude deserts. This adventure promises to be one of a kind and an experience you’ll never forget.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Manali to Jispa via Sissu

Depart from Manali and drive to Sissu village. Explore Sissu waterfalls and the Tandi confluence of Chandra and Bhaga rivers. Continue to Jispa for a luxury stay in our geodesic domes.

Day 2: High Passes & Alpine Lakes

Early breakfast followed by a drive to Baralacha Pass and Surajtaal Lake. Enjoy a picnic lunch in the high-altitude desert. Return to Jispa via Gemur village to experience local Himalayan life.

Day 3: Return to Manali

Enjoy your final breakfast in the domes. Start the return journey, stopping at the iconic Rohtang Pass for scenic views. Arrive in Manali by mid-afternoon.

* This itinerary can be customized as per your interests and preferences.
