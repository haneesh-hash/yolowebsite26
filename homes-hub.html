Let the river tell you to slow down. Let the pines tell you to breathe.

YOLO Homes Kasol sits in the heart of the Parvati Valley at 1,600 metres — a modern sanctuary built for those who crave stillness without sacrificing comfort. Cedar-scented rooms, handpicked linens, an organic garden, and the sound of the Parvati River as your constant companion. We've designed every corner to let the mountains in while keeping you warm, fed, and deeply at ease.
