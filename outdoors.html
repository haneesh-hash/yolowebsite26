Sleep under a billion stars, wake to the Bhaga River, and remember what it feels like to be alive.

YOLO Outdoors is our flagship experience — geodesic domes pitched on the banks of the Bhaga River in Jispa, at 3,200 meters. This is raw Himalayan beauty meets cinematic luxury. Every detail is designed for those who crave the wild but refuse to compromise on comfort. It is the most remote and spectacular property in the YOLO collection, where the landscapes are epic, the silence is absolute, and the night sky will change the way you see the world.

A Day at YOLO Outdoors

From first light to the Milky Way, every moment is cinematic.

5:30 AM

First Light at 3,200m

The peaks turn pink before the sun even rises. Step out of your dome into silence.

7:30 AM

Riverside Breakfast

Hot chai, fresh parathas, and the Bhaga River as your morning soundtrack.

10:00 AM

High-Altitude Trek

Explore the Lahaul Valley — ancient monasteries, hanging glaciers, and endless sky.

1:00 PM

Camp Lunch

Return to camp for a hearty Himalayan lunch. Rest in your dome or read by the river.

3:30 PM

River Walk

Follow the Bhaga downstream. Skip stones. Photograph the blue glacial water.

5:30 PM

Blue Hour

The sky turns electric. The mountains glow. This is why photographers come here.

7:00 PM

Firepit Dinner

Gather around the crackling fire for a multi-course dinner under open sky.

9:00 PM

Milky Way

Zero light pollution. Billions of stars. The Milky Way stretches horizon to horizon.
