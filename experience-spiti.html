About Spiti

Spiti is a remote, high-altitude region in Himachal Pradesh, known for its stunning natural beauty, stark landscapes, and unique cultural heritage. Home to 1000-year-old Buddhist monasteries and deep gorges.

Why Spiti?

Due to its remote location and harsh climate, Spiti is not as well-known or heavily visited as some other regions of India. However, this has helped to preserve its natural and cultural heritage, and has made it a popular destination for travelers seeking a unique and off-the-beaten-path experience.

Whether you’re interested in trekking, photography, or cultural exploration, Spiti is a destination that is sure to captivate and inspire you.
