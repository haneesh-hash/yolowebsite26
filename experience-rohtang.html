Classic Day Trip

Rohtang Pass

The pass that divides two worlds. Just 51km from Manali.

Duration 1 Day
Difficulty Easy
Season May – Oct
Altitude 3,978m

Overview

Where Two Worlds Meet

Rohtang La (3,978m) is the dramatic divide between the lush Kullu Valley and the stark Lahaul high desert. For most people, it is the first snow they have ever touched. Activities: sledging, snowboarding, skiing, photography.

Timeline

Itinerary

5:30am Depart Manali +

Early start non-negotiable. Rohtang generates massive traffic. Permits required online. Note: closed every Tuesday.

8:00am Stops on the Way +

Solang Valley (optional), Gulaba (snow activities), Rahala Falls at 8,500 ft.

10:00am Rohtang La Summit +

Snow-covered. Activities: sledging, snow scooter rides, skiing hire. Views of Sonapani Glacier.

1:00pm Descent +

Have lunch at Marhi — basic but hot food, excellent views.

4:00pm Return +

To Manali or continue directly toward Jispa (add 2hrs).

Key Highlights

What Makes It Special

❄ Snowfields — year-round at summit
🏔 The contrast — green valley behind, barren Lahaul ahead
🏂 Activities: sledging, snow scooter, skiing, photography
🏞 Sonapani Glacier and Dashahr Lake

Be Prepared

Packing Checklist

Essentials

Permit (online, book day before)
Warm jacket / gloves / hat
Waterproof boots (snow is wet)
Snacks and thermos
Important Notes

Closed every Tuesday for maintenance. Opens mid-May, closes October. Only 800 petrol + 400 diesel vehicles permitted daily. Traffic can add 2 hours in peak summer.
