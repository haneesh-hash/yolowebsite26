A soul-stirring multi-day journey through the heart of Parvati Valley. From the 'Magic Village' of Chalal to the high-altitude peace of Tosh, we curate the ultimate Himalayan escape including riverside stays and hidden trail treks.

Brief Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kasol

Check-in at YOLO Kasol Homes. Riverside lunch followed by a gentle walk to the Parvati River. Evening bonfire and social dinner.

Day 2: Chalal & The Magic Village

A gentle 30-minute trek to Chalal. Explore the lush trails and vibrant cafes. Return to Kasol for a relaxed evening.

Day 3: Tosh & Departure

Drive to Tosh, the edge of the valley. Short hike for panoramic views. Check-out and onward journey via Manikaran Sahib.
